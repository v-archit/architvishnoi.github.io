Rotate-a-Cube

Cube Rotation Simulation Experiment
Using Maths, Physics, and Animation

Maths Manipulation

Simulation of cube rotation on the ground using Mathf and RotateAround functions with varying angle speed to change the rotation frequency. The trick is to change the point of rotation after each half rotation for a smooth and fluid transition. Raycasting is employed to check when the face of cube is parallel to the ground and only then it is allowed to change direction.
Tags: Unity3D-C#-Simulation-Maths
Role: Solo Project
Skills: Raycasting, Cube Rotation, Input-to-Action Conversion, Automatic Random Floor Generation

Physics Force

Simulation of cube rotation on the ground using Physics Force and High Friction - Low Bounce surface with varying Drag to keep the cube from flying away. High friction surface restricts cube from slipping and low bounce keeps it on the ground. High Drag further limits it's vertical movement.
Tags: Unity3D-C#-Simulation-Physics-Force-Friction-Drag
Role: Solo Project
Skills: Cube Rotation, Automatic Random Floor Generation, Varying Force

Animation Keyframes

Simulation of cube rotation on the ground using Animation Sequence and Geometrical Curves with varying animation speed to change the rotation frequency. The crucial part was to accurately simulate cube rotation due to it's typical Parabolic movement.
Tags: Unity3D-C#-Simulation-Animation-Dopesheet
Role: Solo Project
Skills: Cube Rotation, Realistic Animation, Automatic Random Floor Generation
