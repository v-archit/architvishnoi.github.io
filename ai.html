The AI Story

2D Narrative Decision-Based
Unity C# Inky Windows

Overview

The AI Story is a 2D narrative driven side-scroller in which the player controls a programmer who has developed an AI at his job which has started to malfunction. The game stimulates the passing of time by a daily conversation between the programmer and AI along with some tasks to gauge the health of AI. The choices affect how the AI reacts and the progression of the game.
Role: Lead Programmer, Production Studio
Skills: Unity3D, UI, Logic, Inky File Management, Dialogue System
Link: The AI Story Github

End Game

End Game starts after you accumulate enough Daily Chats and Daily Mini Game tasks. This marks the beginning of the end of the game when the AI starts malfunctioning and it needs to be dealt with. Player is faced with some important "game changing" choices which leads to a fitting end to this story. Refer to ProgrammedAI handle on twitter to identify the hidden codes and passwords for further progression.
