Introduction / Gestures

It is an e-learning app which provides an immersive experience about the UNESCO Heitage Site of Rani ki Vav. It uses Google ARCore Ground Tracking functionality to augment digital model of Rani ki Vav on the ground. User can interact with this realistic 3D interface with 3 learning objectives - Art, Science, and Architecture.
Tags: Unity3D-C#-Augmented Reality-Google ARCore-Android-eLearning
Role: Skeleton Designer, UI Implementation, Science Experiment Designer
Skills: UI Logic, Science Level Design, Realistic Cloud Simulation, Optimization

Science Experiment / Architecture

Architecture: Showcases the exploded view of the monument complex and its 7 levels.
Archimedes Principle: Demonstrates the Archimedes Principle with the help of an earthen pot immersed in water. As the weight of the pot increases, it gets more submerged in water.
Water Cycle: Simulates the effect of wind and temperature on water level inside the well and humidity in the environment which condenses in the form of clouds and then precipitates.
