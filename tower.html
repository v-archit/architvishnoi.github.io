Tower Defense Prototype (GOAP)

Tower Defense is a protoype which showcases the power of Goal Oriented Action Planning AI in games and how it can be used to make engaging and challenging AI. GOAP AI finds the optimal solution from a vast pool of dynamic states to reach the end goal state. In this game, you have to destroy all the opponent's Towers in order to win, while also creating and maximising the resources. Create combat and siege units to fight, and forager and wood cutters to create resources.
