Food Delivery AI (GOAP)

Food Delivery Simulator showcases the working of Goal Oriented Action Planning AI which finds the optimal solution from a vast pool of dynamic states to reach the end goal state. In this simulator, Food needs to be delivered whenever an order is placed. First, the Bike agent delivers ingredients to the kitchen which the Chef converts into cooked food and then the Car delivers to its final location.
